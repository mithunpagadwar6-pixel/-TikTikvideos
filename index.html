Can't find your TV?

Troubleshooting Tips:
  • Make sure your TV supports casting (Chromecast, AirPlay, or Miracast)
  • Ensure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network
  • Check if your TV's casting feature is enabled
  • Try restarting your TV and router
  • Make sure TikTik app is up to date
Compatible Devices:
  • Smart TVs with built-in casting
  • Chromecast or Chromecast built-in
  • Apple TV (AirPlay)
  • Samsung Smart TV (2016 or later)
  • LG Smart TV (webOS 3.0 or later)
  • Android TV devices
  • Amazon Fire TV
